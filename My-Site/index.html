About The Next Move

"The Next Move" is dedicated to helping new and aspiring board game players learn the fundamentals of various games while developing strategic thinking. Our goal is to make learning games easy, fun, and engaging by providing clear explanations of rules, step-by-step guides, and essential strategies. Whether you're playing for the first time or looking to refine your skills, we aim to be your go-to resource for mastering the games you love.

Settlers of Catan

Catan is a strategic board game where players compete to settle the island of Catan by gathering resources, building roads, settlements, and cities, and trading with others.
